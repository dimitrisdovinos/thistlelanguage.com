Which course is best for my child?

For teenagers we run two types of course. They are General English and Exam English.

General English

Levels: Pre-intermediate, Intermediate, Upper-intermediate, Advanced, Proficiency (B1-C2)
Course length: Four and a half hours per week, 150 hours per school year.

Course description

Our General English courses for teenagers are designed to improve on their previous English knowledge and help them manoeuvre effectively and confidently onto the next level. They are interactive and supportive courses designed to challenge your teen and provide a positive, meaningful learning experience. Courses are developmentally appropriate and are taught with a variety of communicative techniques.

Lesson content is focussed on real-world English, adapted to the needs of your teen, and repeated and recycled. Students in these classes will be encouraged to do group projects and guided towards greater learner autonomy.

- Coursebooks and materials have been chosen and designed to specifically appeal to students of this age group.
- Your child will be continually assessed throughout the course by their teacher and have a monthly progress test.
- All feedback and assessments will be available, privately, to you and your teen on Edmodo.

Students on these courses will be given homework, the details of which will be posted online along with extra resources for self-study.

Exam English

Levels: Intermediate, Upper-intermediate, Advanced, Proficiency (B1+ - C2)
Course length: Six hours per week, 200 hours per school year

Course description

Our Exam English courses for teenagers follow the same objectives as the General English course but with more emphasis placed on exam practice. Lesson content is focussed on the language needed for the exam your teen is sitting. Furthermore, as we near the exam, they will receive exam practice, including speaking tests, with detailed feedback on their performance.

- Coursebooks and materials have been chosen and designed to specifically appeal to students of this age group.
- Your child will be continually assessed throughout the course by their teacher and have a monthly progress test.
- All feedback and assessments will be available, privately, to you and your teen on Edmodo.

Students on these courses will be given homework; the details of which will be posted online along with extra resources for self-study.

warning The cost of the examination is not included in the course costs.
