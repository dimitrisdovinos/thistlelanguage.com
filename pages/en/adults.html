What course is best for me?

We run 3 courses for adult learners of English. General English, Exam English and English for Specific Purposes (ESP).

General English

Levels: Beginner, Elementary, Pre-Intermediate, Intermediate, Upper-intermediate, Advanced, Proficiency (A1 - C2)
Course length: To be arranged

Course description

Our General English course for adults will improve your English language skills and help you communicate more accurately and fluently. It is a communicative, supportive course designed to provide a positive and meaningful learning experience. Lesson content is focussed on real-world English and adapted to your level and needs.

- Coursebooks and materials are chosen and designed specifically for your language requirements.
- You will be continually assessed throughout the course by your teacher and have a monthly progress test.
- All feedback and assessments will be available, privately, to you on Edmodo.

Students on these courses will be given homework, the details of which will be posted online along with extra resources for self-study.

Exam English

Prepare for the IELTS, Cambridge or Michigan exams.

Levels: Intermediate, Upper-intermediate, Advanced, Proficiency (B1+ - C2)
Course length: Six hours per week, 200 hours per school year

Course description

Our Exam English course for adults will improve your English language skills and help you prepare for your chosen exam.

It is a communicative, supportive course designed to provide a positive and meaningful learning experience. Lesson content is focussed on understanding the exam and learning test-taking strategies and techniques. This, along with exam practice and focussed feedback will ensure confidence on your exam day.

- Coursebooks and materials are chosen and designed specifically for your language requirements.
- You will be continually assessed throughout the course by your teacher and have a monthly progress test.
- All feedback and assessments will be available, privately, to you on Edmodo.

Students on these courses will be given homework, the details of which will be posted online along with extra resources for self-study.

warning The cost of the examination is not included in the course costs.
