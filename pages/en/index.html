About us

Welcome to the Thistle School of English, a modern, English language school in the centre of Halkida.
At Thistle we are dedicated to providing the best teaching and learning experience for our students. We offer all levels of general English for all ages (including preparation for all Cambridge and Michigan exams) as well as academic English (preparation for IELTS).
