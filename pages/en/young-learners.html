Which course is best for my child?

My child is 4-5 (pre-school)

For children aged 4-5 we have one course called Pre-Junior.

Levels: Beginner, Elementary (A1)
Course length: One and a half hours per week, 50 hours per school year

Course description

This is a relaxed and stress-free course designed to make new learners feel safe. Students are taught using flashcards, realia, music and chants, games, stories and creative activities. These are all kept fun and short to ensure attention and enjoyment.

Although the emphasis is on students becoming confident with speaking and listening, we will also touch gently on reading and writing.

Continuous recycling of language will allow your child to see for themselves how much they have learned.

Material has been chosen and designed to specifically appeal to students of this age group and even though students on this course will not be formally tested, they will be continuously assessed by their teacher with feedback made available on Edmodo allowing you to keep up with their progress.

There is no assigned homework for this course, however, material will be posted online if parents wish to practise further at home.

My child is 6-11

For children aged 6-11 we have five courses. They are Junior A + B, Senior A, Senior B, Senior C and Senior D.

Levels: Beginner, Elementary, Pre-intermediate, Intermediate (A1 - B1+)
Course length: Three hours per week, 100 hours per school year

Course description

These are relaxed and supportive courses designed to challenge your child and provide a positive, and meaningful learning experience.

Courses are developmentally appropriate and are taught with a variety of techniques using flashcards, realia, music, games, stories and creative activities.

Lesson content is focussed on meaningful, real-world English and repeated and recycled, allowing your child to understand and use the new language in a more natural way.

In these courses students will be encouraged to work together and although reading and writing will be taught, emphasis will be placed on speaking and listening.

- Coursebooks and materials have been chosen and designed to specifically appeal to students of this age group.
- Your child will be continually assessed throughout the course by their teacher and have a monthly progress test.
- All feedback and assessments will be available, privately, to you on Edmodo.

Students on these courses will be given homework; the details of which will be posted online along with extra resources for you to help your child.
