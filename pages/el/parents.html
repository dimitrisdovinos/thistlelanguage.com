At Thistle your child will learn English in a safe, fun and effective environment. Being taught with age-appropriate techniques and material, your child will improve their communication skills and build confidence in their English.

What can you expect from your course?:

  • A highly qualified, experienced teacher.
  • Fun, communicative and effective lessons that will ensure you are happy and making progress.
  • Individual help when required.
  • Daily access to the library for self-study and book lending.
    Your child can visit the library to work on areas outlined by your teacher or take advantage of the extensive English resources offered by Thistle, such as CDs, books, magazines and comics.
  • Private feedback and access to your lesson aims, homework and grades.(Edmodo)
  • Online study help adapted specifically to you. (Edmodo)
  • No hidden costs - materials are included in the price.
  • Coursebooks and materials which are age appropriate.
  • Your child to study with other students of a similar age and level to ensure maximum progress.
  • A progress test given once a month.
  • An end of course certificate showing the course you studied and the dates of that course.
  • An end of course Academic Report detailing your progress and level.

All courses include:

  • Grammar
  • Vocabulary
  • Speaking and pronunciation
  • Writing
  • Reading
  • Listening
